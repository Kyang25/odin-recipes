Chicken Alfredo

Description

Chicken Alfredo is a simple and tasty dish to make at home with simple ingredients.

Recipe

  • Chicken
  • Pasta
  • Alfredo Sauce

    • Steps

    1. Boil the noodles until soft.
    2. pan-fry chicken in oil, butter, and alfredo sauce till temp reaches 165 degrees.
    3. Transfer chicken to cutting board and let sit till colied. Cut into thick slices.
    4. Using same pan making the chicken, add pasta immediately after draining.
    5. Mix and serve
