Garlic Bread

Description

This recipe is for making delicious garlic bread from scratch. There will be a decent amount of ingredients required.

Recipe

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • liive Oil
  • Fresh Garlic
  • Garlic Powder
  • Parsley
  • Salt

    • Steps

    1. Start by preheating oven to 400 degrees.
    2. In a bowl mix butter, olive oil, garlic (minced), garlic powder, chopped parsley, and salt till smooth.
    3. Cut the loaf in half and cut each piece lengthwise. Spread garlic butter mixture evenly over the open surface of the bread.
    4. Bake for 10-15 min, or until brown and crispy. Serve Hot
